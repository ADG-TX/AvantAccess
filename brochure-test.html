Why it matters

In Texas, the threat isn't the drive. It's the storage.

A climate-controlled garage is only as good as the system running it. When that system drifts out of spec, the damage is silent, gradual, and expensive — and it lands on the one asset you can't simply replace.

💧

Humidity & Condensation

Trapped moisture invites surface rust, brake corrosion, and mold on interiors and leather.

🌡️

Heat Swings

An underperforming unit lets temperatures climb, degrading paint, seals, fluids, and electronics.

⚙️

Silent System Decline

Dirty coils and loose connections cut capacity long before the unit ever "fails."

A replacement mini-split system runs $8,000–$15,000. A humidity-damaged collection costs far more. Protecting all of it starts at about $2 a day.
